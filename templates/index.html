    The Cursed Land is a cross-platform Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game (MMORPG) developed by PopuGames for mobile devices and PC. The game bringing together the best of Web 2.0 ,Web 3.0 and AI gaming built on MultiversX. As a free-to-play and play-to-earn game with microtransactions, on-chain token utility and NFT integration
